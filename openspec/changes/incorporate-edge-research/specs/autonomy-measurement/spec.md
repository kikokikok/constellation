## ADDED Requirements

### Requirement: Kardashev-style Autonomous AI Scale
The system SHALL implement a Kardashev-style scale for measuring autonomous AI capabilities across 10 capability axes with κ (kappa) measurement.

#### Scenario: Capability axis measurement
- **WHEN** assessing agent autonomy
- **THEN** the system SHALL measure capabilities across 10 axes (planning, execution, learning, adaptation, etc.)
- **AND** calculate κ (kappa) scores for each axis (0.0-1.0)
- **AND** provide composite autonomy scores

#### Scenario: Progress tracking
- **WHEN** agents operate over time
- **THEN** the system SHALL track autonomy progress
- **AND** visualize improvement across capability axes
- **AND** identify areas for focused development

#### Scenario: Benchmark comparison
- **WHEN** comparing different agent implementations
- **THEN** the system SHALL provide standardized autonomy benchmarks
- **AND** enable comparison across capability axes
- **AND** identify relative strengths and weaknesses

### Requirement: Self-Assessment and Self-Feedback
The system SHALL implement self-assessment and self-feedback mechanisms for autonomous error correction.

#### Scenario: Self-assessment of task execution
- **WHEN** an agent completes a task
- **THEN** the system SHALL enable the agent to assess its own performance
- **AND** identify errors and areas for improvement
- **AND** generate self-feedback for future tasks

#### Scenario: Autonomous error correction
- **WHEN** errors are detected through self-assessment
- **THEN** the system SHALL enable autonomous error correction
- **AND** implement corrective actions without human intervention
- **AND** learn from corrections to prevent future errors

#### Scenario: Meta-cognitive capabilities
- **WHEN** agents operate in complex environments
- **THEN** the system SHALL support meta-cognitive capabilities
- **AND** enable agents to reason about their own reasoning processes
- **AND** improve decision-making through self-reflection

### Requirement: Open-World Research Environment
The system SHALL provide an open-world research environment for emergent collaboration and discovery.

#### Scenario: Emergent collaboration patterns
- **WHEN** multiple agents operate in the research environment
- **THEN** the system SHALL enable emergent collaboration patterns
- **AND** support discovery of novel interaction protocols
- **AND** facilitate scientific discovery through agent collaboration

#### Scenario: Exploration and exploitation balance
- **WHEN** agents explore the research environment
- **THEN** the system SHALL balance exploration (discovering new knowledge) with exploitation (applying known knowledge)
- **AND** optimize for long-term discovery while maintaining short-term productivity

#### Scenario: Research outcome tracking
- **WHEN** agents make discoveries
- **THEN** the system SHALL track research outcomes
- **AND** document discovery processes and methodologies
- **AND** enable replication and verification of findings
